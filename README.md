# FinanceOS Specification

> The official specification, architecture, and product documentation for FinanceOS.

## Overview

FinanceOS is an offline-first personal finance platform designed to help individuals understand, organize, and manage their financial lives with clarity and confidence.

This repository is the authoritative source of truth for the FinanceOS project. It defines the product vision, business rules, architecture, requirements, and engineering decisions that guide every implementation.

The implementation repositories—including the Flutter application and future backend services—must conform to this specification.

## Objectives

FinanceOS aims to:

* Provide a reliable and intuitive personal finance experience.
* Operate offline by default while supporting future synchronization.
* Maintain a clear separation between business rules and implementation.
* Be modular, maintainable, and extensible.
* Serve as a long-term platform rather than a single application.
