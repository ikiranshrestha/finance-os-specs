# Non-Goals

**Version:** 0.1.0 (Draft)

---

FinanceOS is not intended to:

- Replace professional accounting software.
- Provide financial or investment advice.
- Operate as a banking platform.
- Support enterprise financial management.
- Require mandatory cloud services.
- Implement every possible finance feature in the MVP.

These capabilities may be considered in future releases if they align with the product vision.
