**Version:** 0.1.0 (Draft)

# The Problem

Personal financial information is often fragmented across multiple applications, spreadsheets, bank accounts, and manual records.

Existing finance applications frequently prioritize feature count over clarity, require constant internet connectivity, or lock users into proprietary ecosystems.

This fragmentation makes it difficult for individuals to gain a complete understanding of their financial situation.

---

# Our Solution

FinanceOS addresses these challenges by providing a unified, offline-first platform where users can manage, analyze, and plan their finances with confidence.

The platform emphasizes transparency, maintainability, and long-term ownership of financial data.
