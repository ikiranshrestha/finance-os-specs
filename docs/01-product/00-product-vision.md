# Product Vision

**Version:** 0.1.0 (Draft)

---

# Vision

FinanceOS is a modern, offline-first personal finance platform designed to help individuals understand, organize, and improve their financial lives.

Rather than being a simple expense tracker, FinanceOS serves as a complete financial operating system that brings together budgeting, savings, investments, goals, reporting, and future financial services into a unified experience.

The platform is built with long-term maintainability, transparency, and extensibility in mind.

---

# Vision Statement

To become the most trustworthy and extensible personal finance platform by empowering users with complete visibility, control, and understanding of their finances.

---

# Core Values

- User-first
- Offline-first
- Transparency
- Reliability
- Simplicity
- Extensibility
- Security
- Privacy

---

# Long-Term Direction

FinanceOS should evolve into a comprehensive financial platform capable of supporting personal finance needs throughout a user's financial journey while remaining modular, maintainable, and specification-driven.
