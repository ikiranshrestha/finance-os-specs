# Product Goals

**Version:** 0.1.0 (Draft)

---

# Primary Goals

- Help users understand their financial position.
- Simplify personal financial management.
- Support long-term financial planning.
- Operate without requiring internet connectivity.
- Provide reliable and transparent financial calculations.
- Maintain a modular and extensible architecture.

---

# Success Indicators

- High reliability
- Fast performance
- Simple user experience
- Accurate financial reporting
- Sustainable architecture
